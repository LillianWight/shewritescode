Step 1

You'll complete my questionnaire and we'll do some back-and-forth until I know what you need.

Step 2

I'll present you with three Web site options, from basic to premium. You'll choose one and pay a deposit.

Step 3

I'll produce a draft within an agreed-upon time frame, then we'll tweak it until you're ready to see it go live.

Step 4

For at least 30 days after the site goes live I'll be available at no charge to ensure the site is as you want it.
